Instruction Messages Count

Track and manage instruction messages in your chat. Shows active/total count and provides navigation and keep functionality.

Automatically hide instruction messages that are no longer at least second to the last message (excludes kept instructions).

Features

  • Counter Badge: Shows active/total instruction messages (e.g., 1/5)
  • Keep Button: Pin instructions to prevent auto-hiding
  • Navigate Button: Jump to specific instruction messages
  • Force Hide: Manually hide all old non-kept instructions

Usage

Click the list icon in the left send form to open the instruction messages panel. The badge shows the count of active (visible) instructions vs total instructions. Use the pin button to keep important instructions from being auto-hidden.
